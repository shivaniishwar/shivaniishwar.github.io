about me

I'm a professional data communicator based in Brooklyn, New York. I currently attend Pratt Institute, where I will complete my M.S. program in Data Analytics and Visualization, along with an Advanced Certificate in User Experience, in May of 2021. I'm also currently a Digital Analytics Fellow at United Nations Global Pulse.

My goal is to bridge the divide between sciences and humanities. With my unique blend of skills in programming, analysis, communication, and design, I aim to contribute to a world where anyone can understand data, and where science and technology are comprehensible and accessible to all.

When I'm not working, you can find me exploring the city, experimenting in the kitchen, or entertaining a picky little parrot.

my portfolio

Here are some recent examples of my work.

Queer in the Eyes of the Law

An end-to-end data analysis and visualization project that explores the laws governing queerness and how they differ around the world. This project is intended to interpret large, complicated data for an audience unfamiliar with queer rights issues.

TOOLS USED: Google Sheets, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, D3

Beauty Dos and Don'ts

A comparative analysis of five cosmetic companies' Twitter habits, intended to highlight successful strategies for maintaining a social media presence in the beauty industry.

TOOLS USED: SuperMetrics, Google Sheets, Tableau, Adobe InDesign

The Speech of Star Wars

An exploratory data analysis project analyzing the scripts of the original Star Wars trilogy, including word frequency, sentiment analysis, and topic modeling. This project is intended to introduce and explain various common methods of text analysis.

TOOLS USED: R, RStudio

Music My Way

A data cleaning and visualization project exploring my personal music-downloading habits on Spotify. This project highlights tools used to gather, clean, and display data.

TOOLS USED: Spotify API, OpenRefine, Tableau
