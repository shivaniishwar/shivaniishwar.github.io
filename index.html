about me

I'm a professional data communicator based in Brooklyn, New York. I currently attend Pratt Institute, where I will complete my M.S. program in Data Analytics and Visualization, along with an Advanced Certificate in User Experience, in May of 2021. My goal is to bridge the divide between sciences and humanities. With my unique blend of skills in programming, analysis, communication, and design, I aim to contribute to a world where anyone can understand data, and where science and technology are comprehensible and accessible to all.

When I'm not working, you can find me exploring the city, experimenting in the kitchen, or entertaining a picky little parrot.
